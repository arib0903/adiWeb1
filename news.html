Well here's a game I created using the unity game engine(Works only on a laptop or the like)

  • Hold space to fly
  • Press the "A" key, on your keypad, to tilt left
  • Press the "D" key, on your keypad, to tilt right
  • Objective: to land on the green pad
  • FYI: From the first level the spaceship cannot be seen. Please accelerate and tilt right to be visible in frame
  • TIP: Don't hit anything! AGH, you will get the gist in a bit, just play it 50 time. Also, Please stay in frame
